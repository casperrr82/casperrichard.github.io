About Me

I am Casper Richard, and I orchestrate product symphonies.

Back in 2007, I began in customer service, learning to listen and understand what people need. This experience sparked my interest in making products that truly help people, not just fixing temporary issues.

In marketing, I used email campaigns to make our brand more popular. I always focus on what users need, talking to them and using their feedback to improve how we tell them about our products. Managing projects, I’ve made sure things are done well and quickly, like speeding up client setups by 20%. I look at data to guide our product choices and marketing.

I’ve just finished a Product Management course at York University, which has improved my skills. I’m always learning, attending events, and joining online groups. My aim is to make products that solve problems and make people’s lives better.
